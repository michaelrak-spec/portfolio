01 — About

Operator by title, builder by instinct.

I lead People Operations and People Analytics at a fast-scaling consumer wellness company, where I'm responsible for the systems that keep a growing, multi-state team running — onboarding and offboarding, payroll, compliance, headcount analytics, and the day-to-day infrastructure of the people function.

What sets my work apart is that I build the tooling myself. When a process is manual, brittle, or invisible, I prototype the fix and ship it — from full-stack web apps with real authentication and databases, to live dashboards that pull from our actual systems, to automation that eliminates repetitive work.

I'm fluent across the stack a modern operator needs: SQL and data analysis, full-stack development (Next.js, React, Postgres), product thinking, and AI-assisted engineering. I care about clean systems, secure handling of sensitive people data, and tools that other people actually want to use.

I've done this across the full company spectrum — from sub-50-person, venture-backed startups (Fathom Radiant, Mosey) to a public company (Datto, through its NYSE IPO and $6.2B acquisition) and a hypergrowth brand now inside Unilever. So I'm equally at home moving fast on a scrappy startup team and operating with the structure and rigor a large corporation demands.

02 — Selected work

Things I've built.

A sample of internal products I designed and shipped — each one solving a real operational problem for a real team. Details generalized to protect company data.

🗓️
Full-stack app

Company Offsite Planning Platform

A production web app — shipped across ~50 iterations — that runs a 120-person company offsite end to end: RSVPs, dietary & swag, travel, budgets, vendor outreach, an agenda builder and run-of-show, plus auto-grouping that clusters attendees into airport shuttles by flight-arrival windows. Role-based access gives the planning team a full admin suite and every employee a personalized participant portal.

Integrated the Google Maps Platform (Maps JavaScript + Places APIs) for hotel/airport autocomplete and an interactive, color-coded POI map; sends on-brand HTML emails with inline (CID) hero images; and persists to Google Cloud Storage with optimistic-concurrency (compare-and-swap) writes. Deployed on Cloud Run behind Identity-Aware Proxy, with a server-side Places cache + photo proxy to protect the API key.

Live workspace

Unified People Ops Workspace

A live operator workspace that pulls in real time from six business systems — Slack, email, calendar, Asana, Gusto, and Ramp — into one Inbox / Queue / Team view. It evolved from my own daily dashboard into a shareable, zero-data team app shell any teammate can personalize to their role and connectors, with privacy designed so each viewer only ever sees their own data.

Probed every API for its real response shape, then shipped a self-contained, multi-user tool with strict per-user data access.
