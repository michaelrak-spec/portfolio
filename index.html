01 — About

Operator by title, builder by instinct.

I lead People Operations and People Analytics at a fast-scaling consumer wellness company, where I'm responsible for the systems that keep a growing, multi-state team running — onboarding and offboarding, payroll, compliance, headcount analytics, and the day-to-day infrastructure of the people function.

What sets my work apart is that I build the tooling myself. When a process is manual, brittle, or invisible, I prototype the fix and ship it — from full-stack web apps with real authentication and databases, to live dashboards that pull from our actual systems, to automation that eliminates repetitive work.

I'm fluent across the stack a modern operator needs: SQL and data analysis, full-stack development (Next.js, React, Postgres), product thinking, and AI-assisted engineering. I care about clean systems, secure handling of sensitive people data, and tools that other people actually want to use.

I've done this across the full company spectrum — from sub-50-person, venture-backed startups to a public company (through a NYSE IPO and $6.2B acquisition) and a hypergrowth brand now inside a global enterprise. So I'm equally at home moving fast on a scrappy startup team and operating with the structure and rigor a large corporation demands.
